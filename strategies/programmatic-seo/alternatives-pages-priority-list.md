## Competitor Research Notes

### Tableau
- Pain points: Expensive, requires Prep for data cleaning, steep learning curve
- Their pricing: ~$70/user/month
- Key gaps: No AI insights, manual analysis only

### Power BI
- Pain points: Microsoft ecosystem lock-in, DAX complexity, limited Mac support
- Their pricing: ~$10-20/user/month (but enterprise features costly)
- Key gaps: AI features require premium, complex for non-technical users

### Looker
- Pain points: Requires LookML expertise, expensive, Google dependency
- Their pricing: Custom (typically $$$)
- Key gaps: Developer-focused, not self-service friendly

### ThoughtSpot
- Pain points: Expensive, complex deployment, enterprise sales cycle
- Their pricing: Custom (enterprise only)
- Key gaps: Heavy infrastructure requirements

### Domo
- Pain points: Expensive, complex, feature bloat
- Their pricing: Custom (typically $$$)
- Key gaps: Overwhelming for smaller teams
