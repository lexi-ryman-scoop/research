# [Competitor] Alternatives Page Template

## Target Keyword Pattern
`[Competitor] alternatives` (e.g., "Tableau alternatives", "Power BI alternatives")

## Search Intent
High-intent users who:
- Are frustrated with current tool's limitations
- Evaluating options before purchase
- Looking to switch from existing solution
- Comparing pricing/features

## Content Guidelines

### Tone
- Objective and helpful (not salesy)
- Acknowledge competitor strengths honestly
- Focus on fit, not "best"
- Use real differentiators

### Word Count
- 2,000-3,000 words optimal
- Long enough for depth, short enough to scan

### Updates
- Review quarterly for pricing/feature changes
- Update "2025" year in January
